About Me

Hey, I’m Devin Wolfenberger! I specialize in UI/UX design and enjoy providing an optimized User Experience. I’m skilled with Adobe Creative Cloud, Figma, UI/UX Design and user centric thinking. I’m a UI/UX Designer with more than 5 years of experience under my belt, and over a decade of experience in Customer Service.

I’m a passionate designer focused on providing an optimized User Experience and designing to create user inclusive environments.

All my life I’ve given attention to detail in everything I do, this has carried over into my passion for design today. Growing up I spent hours consuming illustrations in books, drawing on any loose paper I could find, and eventually creating digital illustrations of my own. In my free time I enjoy outdoor activities with my wife, scrolling Reddit or having a good conversation.
